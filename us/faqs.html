Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our software services, web development, mobile applications, and more.

Software development is the process of creating custom applications and IT systems to solve specific problems. It is important because it allows you to optimize processes, improve productivity, and provide tailored solutions for your business needs.

If your business needs an online presence accessible from any device, a website is ideal. However, if your service requires specific mobile functionalities or constant access via mobile devices, an app might be the best choice. We can help you decide what suits you best.

Digital marketing is key to reaching a wider audience through the internet. It allows you to promote your business, increase brand visibility, and generate more sales opportunities through strategies like social media, SEO, paid advertising, and more.

At **SoftDevAgency**, we offer a variety of services, including mobile app development, custom website creation, security system design, digital marketing, and technical support to ensure your technology always performs at its best.

The development time depends on the project's complexity. A simple website or basic app can take a few weeks, while more complex projects may take several months. Our team works efficiently to deliver solutions on time.

Software maintenance includes updates, bug fixes, performance improvements, and adaptations to new technological requirements to ensure your systems remain functional and secure.

Responsive design ensures that your website looks and functions correctly on any device, whether a computer, tablet, or mobile phone, enhancing user experience and accessibility.

We implement advanced security measures, such as encryption, secure authentication, and regular vulnerability testing, to protect the confidentiality and integrity of your data.

We offer solutions in web development, applications, and more, committed to providing high-quality services for your business.
